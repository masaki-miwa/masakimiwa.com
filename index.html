Masaki Miwa　is a Designer, Design & Art Director who creates frameworks, ecosystems, and narratives from a central concept that addresses a core message, question, or statement.

Each design decision follows the concept to allow exploration and expansion of possibilities and boundaries; towards forms and methods of communication* – informed by subject matter, context, narratives, socio-cultural values, behaviours, and languages.

This methodology expresses and defines a purpose, proposes a question, or shares a viewpoint which creates conversations in search of defining the reason for existence, answers, and connections.
