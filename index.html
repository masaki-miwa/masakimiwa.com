Masaki Miwa is a Designer, Creative & Art Director with a conceptual and interdisciplinary approach to design and direction that create meaningful connections and explore 'beauty of shadows'.
Focusing on design and direction for identities, imagery, objects, printed, spatial, digital to audio-visual projects in the fields of art, design, fashion and contemporary culture.

His methodology explores connection with a sense of time: research (of the past), interpretation and re-interpretation (in the present), crafting narratives and platforms that build a whole and continuing story (for the future). Grounded in conceptual thinking and interdisciplinary references that reflect and create core values permeating across mediums and platforms, as a means to create meaningful connections for the human senses and towards the future.
