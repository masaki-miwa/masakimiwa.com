Masaki Miwa is a Graphic Designer and Art Director with a conceptual approach to design and direction that reflect core values and craft narrative that builds a whole story. Focusing on design and creative direction for visual identities, imagery, printed, spatial and digital projects in the fields of art, design, fashion and contemporary culture.

Currently at Feng Chen Wang, a London based menswear brand as their Brand, Graphic & Digital Designer.
